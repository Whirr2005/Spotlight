Premium Stalls

Permanent built up "shop front"

This style of market stall, allowing a professional setup for merchants to stand out against the crowd. This works well for caterers and vendors who want to grow their brand

Long term rental lets

For premium stalls we allow vendors to sign onto long term let agreements, starting at 6 month lets. We also boast a zero rental increase policy for the length of the first term.

Custom Signage Space

Grow your business or build your brand by showing the customers who you are! Our premium market stands have dedicated space for your branding and signs.

Extra customisation

The Premium Stalls are to be professionally painted and customised upon request to fit your branding and desired.

Greater Customer Draw

Stand out amongst the crowd in a premium stall. The premium stalls draw eyes more then a standard stall giving you more chances to gain sales

Increased Size

The premium stalls are sized 4x4m - 4.5x4.5m this extra space can go a long way compared to the standard 3x3 space for temporary stalls.

Temporary Markets

Bring your own setup

More aligned with standard outdoor markets, allowing vendors to bring their own setups. Works well for merchandise and small goods sellers

Get creative!

With the temporary spaces you can get the creative juices flowing and find ways to make your stand catch the eye of the public

A market with something for everyone.

Indoor community space with rotating market stalls

A space out of the unpredictable UK weather, allowing merchants to get noticed and customers to enjoy a fun day out with various offerings at spotlight

Indoor attractions and Activities

At spotlight you can find plenty to do in the upstairs bar / events hall. Games from beer pong to axe throwing to arcade machines and indoor golf simulators. This brings in potential customers whether they plan on having a browse or not!

Café & pizza kitchen

Downstairs working alongside the markets will be our café and pizzeria team members, here you can find quality pizzas and a well brewed coffee. Relax, get some work done, catch up with old friends. The opportunities are endless
